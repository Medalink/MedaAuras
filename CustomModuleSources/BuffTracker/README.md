`MedaAurasCustomModule.lua` is the file intended for MedaAuras' custom-module importer.

Use it one of two ways:

1. Open `/mwa import` in-game and paste the raw contents of [`MedaAurasCustomModule.lua`](F:\Dev\MedaAuras\CustomModuleSources\BuffTracker\MedaAurasCustomModule.lua), then install it as a custom module.
2. After installing it on your own client, open the new custom module page in MedaAuras and use `Export Code` or `Export + Settings` to generate a share string for other players.

`Module.lua` and `Options.lua` were created by the Ellesmere scaffold generator and kept here as reference-only source material. They are not wired into `MedaAuras.toc`.
